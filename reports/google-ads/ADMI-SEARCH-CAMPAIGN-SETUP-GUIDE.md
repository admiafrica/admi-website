## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

1. **Pin headlines strategically:**

   - Pin H1 (keyword headline) to Position 1
   - Pin H2 (intake/urgency) to Position 2
   - Let Google test remaining headlines

2. **Use Dynamic Keyword Insertion (DKI):**

   - {KeyWord:Default Text} capitalizes first word
   - {Keyword:Default Text} capitalizes each word
   - Always provide default text for long keywords

3. **Monitor Quality Score weekly:**

   - Aim for 7+ quality score
   - Low scores = poor ad relevance or landing page experience
   - Improve by matching ad copy to keywords

4. **Create custom landing pages:**

   - Each ad group should link to specific course page
   - Add intake dates prominently
   - Include clear CTA button

5. **A/B test ad copy:**
   - Test urgency vs. benefit-focused headlines
   - Test different CTAs (Enroll Now vs. Apply Today vs. Get Started)
   - Keep winning ads, pause losers after 2 weeks
