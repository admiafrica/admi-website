## Learnings from January 2026 Campaign

### What Worked ‚úÖ
1. **Quality over quantity** - 3.9% conversion vs 0.2% historical
2. **Performance Max** - 28.5% hot leads (highest quality)
3. **Pre-qualification form** - Reduced low-quality leads
4. **Lead scoring** - Enabled prioritization by admissions team

### What to Improve üîß
1. **First-touch attribution** - Fixed mid-campaign, apply from day 1
2. **Google Search Ads** - Only 0.65% pipeline progress, needs better keywords
3. **Meta Ads to applications** - 0% applications (last-touch), improve nurture
4. **Organic attribution** - 0 leads tracked, investigate GA setup

## Audience Strategy

### Primary Audiences

**1. High School Leavers (Age 17-20)**
- Just completed KCSE/A-Levels
- Looking for creative career paths
- Parents involved in decision
- Target: March-April (results season)

**2. Career Changers (Age 21-30)**
- Working professionals seeking creative careers
- Higher intent, shorter decision cycle
- Can self-finance
- Target: Entire campaign

**3. Gap Year Students (Age 18-22)**
- Deferred university or didn't get placement
- Looking for alternatives
- Parents involved
- Target: Early campaign

### Remarketing Audiences (From January)
- January leads who didn't apply
- Website visitors (90-day)
- Video viewers (50%+ completion)
- Form abandoners
- Course page visitors
