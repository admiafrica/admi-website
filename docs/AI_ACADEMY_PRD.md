# ADMI AI Academy - Product Requirements Document

## Executive Summary

The ADMI AI Academy represents a strategic pivot from infrastructure-dependent education to an agile, outcome-focused career transformation platform. By targeting the intersection of AI technology and creative/business applications, ADMI can leverage its proven track record while entering a high-growth market segment with minimal competition.

## Vision & Mission

### Vision

To become Africa's leading AI career transformation platform, empowering creative and business professionals to harness AI for career advancement and economic opportunity.

### Mission

Deliver accessible, outcome-focused AI education that transforms careers through practical skills, real-world projects, and personalized learning paths.

## Strategic Positioning

### Market Opportunity

- **Target Market**: Creative professionals, marketers, business analysts, entrepreneurs
- **Pain Point**: Traditional AI education is too technical; creative/business education lacks AI integration
- **Unique Value**: Practical AI applications for non-technical professionals with proven career outcomes

### Competitive Advantages

1. **Infrastructure-Light**: Online-first model reduces overhead by 70%
2. **Outcome-Focused**: Career transformation metrics, not just completion rates
3. **Niche Positioning**: AI for creatives/business vs. general AI/ML training
4. **Proven Brand**: ADMI's established reputation in creative education

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Course Fees**

   - Individual programs: $800-1,500
   - Full path access: $2,500-4,000
   - Corporate training: $5,000-15,000 per cohort

2. **Subscription Model**

   - Monthly: $99/month (continuous learning access)
   - Annual: $999/year (20% discount)
   - Includes all micro-courses and tool updates

3. **Career Services**

   - Resume/portfolio review: $200
   - Career coaching: $150/hour
   - Job placement fee: 10% of first year salary

4. **Enterprise Solutions**

   - Custom training programs
   - Team licenses
   - White-label offerings

5. **Marketplace Fees**
   - Project marketplace: 15% commission
   - Tool affiliate revenue
   - Partnership commissions

## Risk Analysis

### Risks & Mitigation

1. **Market Risk**: AI education market saturation

   - **Mitigation**: Niche focus (creative/business), proven brand

2. **Technology Risk**: Rapid AI tool changes

   - **Mitigation**: Agile curriculum updates, tool-agnostic principles

3. **Competition Risk**: Big tech enters market

   - **Mitigation**: Personalization, career services, community

4. **Operational Risk**: Instructor quality/availability

   - **Mitigation**: Recorded content + live Q&A, practitioner network

5. **Financial Risk**: Low enrollment
   - **Mitigation**: Pilot validation, referral incentives, corporate sales
